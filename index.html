A short background of Chinue Achebe

Chinue Achebe was a nigerian novelist who is widely known for his novel Things Fall Apart. He was born on the 16th of November 1930 in Nnobi, a place near his hometown Ogidi.His father, Isaiah Okafo Achebe was a teacher and evangelist while his mother Janet Anaechi helped him on the spread of the the gospel. He (Chinue Achebe) was raised in a wonderful religious family. Considering his parents were among the first converts who embraced Christianity.

He was a brilliant Student while in school such that his teachers could easily notice him.It was this briliance that made him a way to the university college of Ibadan, a branch of the London university. He was enrolled as medical Student but later on changed his mind to study English, history and theology. This was influenced by the desire to show the educate the world about african culture and religion.

He wrote a number of pieces as a Student. This include among others Polar Undergraduate.It was during his time at the Nigerian Broadcasting Corporation (where he worked as a a script writer) when he discovered the famous novel, Things Fall Apart.
